Your payment page is working and creating payment intents, but the verification is failing because the SECRET KEY is not set correctly in your .env file.

You have the public key: pk_live_51OIZMDGpTLDx2Mnr...

But the secret key shows: sk_live_YOUR_SECRET_KEY_HERE (placeholder)

Step 1: Go to Stripe Dashboard

For LIVE payments (real money), visit:

https://dashboard.stripe.com/apikeys

Make sure the toggle at the top shows "Viewing live data"

Step 2: Find Your Secret Key

On that page, you'll see:

  • Publishable key - starts with pk_live_ (you already have this)
  • Secret key - starts with sk_live_ (this is what you need)

Click the "Reveal live key" button next to the Secret key

Copy the entire key that starts with sk_live_

Step 3: Update Your .env File

Open: c:\QuranAcademy\.env

Replace this line:

STRIPE_SECRET_KEY=sk_live_YOUR_SECRET_KEY_HERE

With your actual key (should look something like):

STRIPE_SECRET_KEY=sk_live_XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX

Step 4: Restart Your Application

After saving the .env file, restart the Flask application.
